## Performance findings & design inputs

| **KV-cache precision is a two-regime lever (long context).** On the Arc 140V: at production's short context (≤\~12K tokens) FP16 KV is fastest and INT8 is neutral-to-slightly-slower (dequant overhead); at long context (32K) attention is memory-bandwidth-bound, so INT8 KV is **2.3× faster** and **halves** the KV footprint.
