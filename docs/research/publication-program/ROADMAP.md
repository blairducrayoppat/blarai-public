## 4. Cross-cutting venue facts the surveys established (primary-sourced unless flagged)

- **IAPP (both P4 and P5 confirmed):** hard rule — no content "created, in whole or in
  part, by a generative AI tool without the IAPP's prior written consent." Paths:
  LA-human-authored variant (recommended for P5 — it embodies the thesis) or prior
  written consent. Pitch-first (writeforus@iapp.org), 800–1,200 words.
- **Hacker News:** normal link submissions only (Show HN disqualified for narratives
  and blog posts — two independent rule clauses); no gratuitous numbers in titles; no
  solicited votes; "don't use HN primarily for promotion."
- **LessWrong:** first post human-reviewed; sub-5-karma rate limits; frontpage is
  moderator-decided ("useful, novel, timeless"; explain-not-persuade); crossposts must
  be adapted to the culture; the LLM-judge and oversight debates live there (P3/P5
  discovery surface).
- **Reddit (r/LocalLLaMA, r/programming): NOT on the approved venue list** (LA
  Decision c, 2026-07-19 — he named home base + HN + LessWrong + coordinated channels;
  Reddit was not included). Background for any future revisit: rules pages are
  TOOL-BLOCKED from this environment on every route tried; any future Reddit action
  needs a fresh LA approval AND an in-browser rules read first; r/LocalLLaMA reportedly
  bans undisclosed LLM-generated content (T3-sourced; confirm in-browser).
- **Hugging Face community blog:** clean mechanics, but personal-namespace publishing
  requires a paid PRO subscription (or org membership) — small standing decision.
- **OpenVINO Medium/blog:** editor-curated; engagement-first coordination with DevRel
  (matches our doctrine); Discussion #36484 proves the door is already open.
- **ACM Queue:** likely invitation-only (two independent snippets of the primary page;
  page itself 403'd) — treat as aspirational. **IEEE S&P:** pitch a practitioner
  DEPARTMENT, not a feature (exact mechanics page JS-blocked; verify at author time).
- **BSides:** per-city CFPs; genuinely first-timer-friendly tracks exist (BSidesLV
  Proving Ground mentorship). A talk needs a live-reproduction/tool hook.
- **Workshops (P5 stretch):** AI CHAOS! series = best thesis fit (both 2026 editions
  PAST; watch for 2027 CFP). FAccT 2027: abstract Oct 27 2026 / paper Nov 3 2026,
  non-archival option. AIES 2026 closed.
- **XDA-class outlets:** no guest posts (confirmed from their own pages) — pickup
  only; do not pitch.
